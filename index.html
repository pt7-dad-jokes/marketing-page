Introduction

Hi, welcome to the app, Dad Jokes. This app was created by a bunch of professionals so those who enjoy dad jokes can come and share their interests and is for all ages. All of the best jokes are gathered here and saved onto the app so others can enjoy them too.

Features

This app is easily accessible and easy to use. In the app, you can add and delete jokes. There is a function to favorite jokes you find most amusing. You can also like or dislike jokes, those that you dislike will no longer show up on the app.
